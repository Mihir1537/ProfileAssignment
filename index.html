Project Idea

Overview:

My project idea is to develop a free downloadable smartphone app called Play and Date. This will be an interactive platform where parents can help children build their social life within a safe and secure environment. Parents that are too busy working and don’t want to leave their children home alone can benefit from this app. Using this app will simplify the process of arranging children to play together and to create plans with friends and family, as it provides numerous useful features. Overall, the purpose of this project is to provide parents with the solution to avoid leaving their children home on their own.

Motivation:

“A recent U.S Census report shows 7 million of the nation’s 38 million children ages 5 to 14 are left home alone regularly.” Children who are not supervised tend to show high levels of anxiety, stress, and loneliness. They are also at a much larger risk to be involved in accidents and to be victimized by strangers. Using Play and Date is a quick and easy solution to this problem. This app provides safety and allows parents to go to work without constantly worrying about their children. This application is also useful as it provides an easier method to manage scheduling and allows parents to make better and safer arrangements for their children.

Description:

This application will allow users to experience social interaction with their loved ones in an exciting and entertaining way, as it includes many intriguing features. It will have a calendar which will display the days when the parents are available. Users will also be able to display important information, such as home address, phone numbers and an emergency contact list. This feature is provided for safety reasons, so in the event of an emergency, the parent responsible for taking care of the children will have access to these details. However, it is also understandable if some users feel uncomfortable putting private details online, so there will be security measurements put in place such as a secure password and the ability to choose who can see and access certain information, this will help prevent unauthorised access to sensitive information. The aim of this app is for children to be in a safe social environment; therefore, parents will have the ability to create tables and diagrams filled with important information that is necessary for their children, and the ability to add this crucial information to their accounts/pages. For example, parents have the option of creating a list of allergies to inform and alert the other users, which encourages safe social interaction and helps prevent dangerous situations. This feature can also be utilised to inform other parents about their child’s favourite activities, this could include their favourite books, movies, sports, board games, etc. GPS tracking will also include in this app, which allows users to know how far the other individual is and how long it is approximately going to take to reach their destination. The additional feature of the GPS system helps prevent accidents on the road by restricting texting while driving, and as a result prevents alarming situations. However, if the user feels that their privacy is being breached, the app is quite flexible, and they can either turn their location on or off. This app will also allow users to access their camera and photos through the app. It will include a variety of fun filters, which users can utilise to design their own accounts filled with exciting photos. Play and Date will have a scrapbooking feature, where parents can store the photos of their children having fun with their friends. Although Play and Date will be a free downloadable app, there will still be as subscription fee that will require users to pay $5 dollars per month. However, new users will have the opportunity to subscribe for a free month trial. This will allow parents to take this app for a test run and decide if they would like to continue this fun and safe experience. On top of all that, Play and Date will provide customer service relating to any questions or concerns that customers might come across relating to the app. On this app, customer service will be taken very seriously as it is vital to build trust with the users.

Tools and Technologies:

The tool required for this application is Appy Pie. Appy Pie is an app development software that enables users to create mobile applications compatible with operating systems such as IOS and Android. Appy Pie is quite beneficial for this application as it simplifies the task of creating the smartphone application. It also provides the ability to write HTML and CSS, which can be used to build IOS, and Android based mobile applications. Appy Pie automatically provides flexibility, as it includes many features. Appy Pie is simple, helpful, and easily accessible, therefore I believe this software is the best tool to use to create my application.

  • Security skills: It is important to have sensitive and private information of the users to be protected
